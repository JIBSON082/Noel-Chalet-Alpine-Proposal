Proposal 01 : Manual Payment

Proposal 01  ·  Standard Package

Website Development
Manual Payment Integration

A complete, professional web presence for Noël Chalet Alpine. Students enroll, make bank transfers, confirm via WhatsApp, and the coach grants access to course materials. Simple, trusted, and built for how the business already operates.

Investment ₦750,000
Timeline 5 Weeks
Maintenance 12 Months Free
Domain Included
01

Introduction

This proposal outlines the scope, design, and investment required to build a professional, full-stack website for Noël Chalet Alpine: a French language coaching business serving both Nigerian-based and international students.

The website will establish a strong digital presence, streamline student enrollment, protect course resources, and position Noël Chalet Alpine as a credible, premium institution in French language education. This package uses a straightforward manual payment approach, keeping costs accessible and the workflow familiar for the coaching team.

02

Pages and Structure

The website will consist of seven fully designed and developed pages, each serving a distinct purpose in the student journey.

Home

Hero section with bold headline and Paris aesthetic. Quick stats covering students coached, exams passed, and countries reached. Featured program cards, testimonials strip, and a final call to action.

About

Coach profile, credentials, and personal story. The vision and mission behind Noël Chalet Alpine, written to build trust with prospective students.

Programs and Services

Individual cards for each program: TEF, DELF/DALF, TFI, IGCSE, TEF CANADA, Travels and Tours, General French Fluency. Each card shows coverage, duration, price, and an enroll button.

Pricing

Clean pricing cards per program with enrollment buttons. All prices displayed clearly, with account details surfacing on selection.

Resources (Gated)

Accessible only after payment is confirmed by the coach. Contains audio-visuals, grammar drills, reading materials, and assignments. Protected by Supabase authentication and row-level security.

Virtual Classes

Weekly schedule showing program name, day, time, and platform (Zoom or Google Meet). A registration form collects student details and notifies the coach via email, who then sends the class link manually.

Contact

WhatsApp CTA button that opens a pre-filled message to the admin, alongside a standard contact form for general enquiries.

03

Payment Flow

This proposal uses a manual bank transfer approach, designed to align with how Nigerian students and the coaching team already operate. There are no transaction fees and no third-party payment platform required.

1

Student selects a program

The student browses the Programs or Pricing page and clicks the enroll button for their chosen course.

2

Account details are displayed

The business bank account details (bank name, account number, account name) are clearly shown for the student to copy and make a transfer.

3

Student confirms payment

After making the transfer, the student clicks the "I have sent the payment" button. A prompt appears with instructions to send proof of payment to the admin.

4

WhatsApp evidence link opens

A deep link automatically opens WhatsApp with the admin's number and a pre-filled message including the program name, ready for the student to attach their payment screenshot.

5

Coach verifies and grants access

The coach confirms payment and updates the student record in the Supabase dashboard. The student receives login credentials and can access their gated resources immediately.

03

Payment Flow

Flutterwave powers a fully automated payment experience. Students pay in their preferred currency and receive instant access to their purchased resources. The coach is notified of every successful enrollment automatically.

1

Student selects a program

The student browses the Programs or Pricing page and clicks the enroll button for their chosen course.

2

Flutterwave checkout opens

A secure Flutterwave payment modal appears. The student pays using their card, bank transfer, or mobile money in NGN, USD, EUR, GBP, CAD, or any supported currency.

3

Webhook fires on successful payment

Flutterwave sends a secure webhook notification to the website's API endpoint, confirming the payment and the specific program purchased.

4

Database is updated automatically

The Next.js API route receives the webhook, validates it, and updates the student's enrollment record in Supabase. Access is granted instantly, with no manual action from the coach.

5

Student accesses resources immediately

The student is redirected to their gated Resources page for the purchased program. All materials are available immediately upon payment confirmation.
